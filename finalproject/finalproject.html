Home

Welcome to Hiking Trails of Panama! Explore lush rainforests, volcanic peaks, and breathtaking waterfalls through our carefully curated trail guides. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there’s something here for you.

About

Our mission is to inspire people to experience the beauty of Panama through hiking. We share detailed trail maps, safety tips, and community insights. This site was created to promote eco-tourism and the preservation of Panama’s natural wonders.

Trails

Discover some of the best trails in Panama, including:

  • Volcán Barú Trail: Hike to the highest point in Panama and catch sunrise views of both oceans.
  • Pipeline Trail: A family-friendly rainforest path with rich birdlife and waterfalls.
  • Lost Waterfalls Trail: Explore hidden cascades deep in the cloud forests of Boquete.
