About Nigeria

Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa and is located in West Africa along the Gulf of Guinea. It is known for its diverse cultures, languages, and traditions, with over 500 different ethnic groups. Nigeria is rich in natural resources, particularly oil and gas, and has a rapidly growing tech industry. The country is also famous for its Nollywood film industry, Afrobeat music, and delicious cuisine such as jollof rice and suya.
